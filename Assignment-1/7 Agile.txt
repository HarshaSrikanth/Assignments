Stages of agile methodology:
1.Create Epic and Story.
2.Create Scrum team- (product owner,scrum master,scrum team).
3.Create sprint plan- (who has to complete,assigned to who,effort time/story point are discussed).
4.Managing scrum/standup/syncup calls daily on sprints.
5.Burndownn chart.
6.Retrospection meeting.
7.Repeat 3-4 stages for next sprints.
