Steps to create sprint in JIRA:
1.Create a company managed project in jira software.
2.Now choose a template like kanban/Scrum for next step.
3.Raise a issue by selecting epic/story/task type.
4.Check you backlogs and start a sprint.
5.From that sprint ,now start managing your sprints by selecting,
  what is done,what to do today,what has to be done.
